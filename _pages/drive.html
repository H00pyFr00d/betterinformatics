BetterInformatics Drive Shutdown

BetterInformatics Google Drive is shutting down in spring 2024.

Please migrate any files that you find useful on the Drive to BetterInformatics File Collection: files.betterinformatics.com

With that in mind, you can still access the files you're after here.
Individual files should be publicly accessible without Google sign-in. Contact admins if you have any issues.

With that in mind, you can still access the Drive to view or migrate old documents. As a technical limitation of the Google Drive, you must go through a manual registration process (this is one of the reasons we are no longer using Drive), please email admin@betterinformatics.com following these steps:

  1. write "Better Informatics: please add me as a contributor to Google Drive" as your subject,
  2. send the email from your university email address, so we know you are a student, and
  3. include the email address of your Google account, so that you actually get access to the Drive.

Once you are a member, please click here to access the Shared Drive .
